About the Game

Mythical Realms is a turn-based strategy city builder set on a procedurally generated hex grid. Choose from three distinct races, expand your territory, research technologies, and outmaneuver an AI opponent — all while a dynamic AI storyteller shapes the narrative around your decisions.
